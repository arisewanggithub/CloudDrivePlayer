# Privacy Policy for Cloud Drive Player

**Last Updated:** February 11, 2026

## Introduction

Cloud Drive Player ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Cloud Drive Player stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

1. **Music Library Metadata**
   - File names, artist names, album names, song titles
   - File paths within your cloud storage
   - Play counts, last played timestamps
   - Favorite status and playlist membership

2. **Playback State**
   - Current queue and playback position
   - Playback speed and repeat/shuffle settings
   - Cache of recently played tracks

3. **App Settings**
   - Selected cloud provider (OneDrive or Google Drive)
   - Music folder selections
   - Cache size preferences
   - Offline folder selections

4. **Authentication Tokens**
   - Temporary access tokens for OneDrive or Google Drive
   - Stored securely using Android's encrypted storage

### Data Collected via Firebase

Cloud Drive Player uses Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to improve app stability and performance:

**Firebase Crashlytics collects:**
- Crash reports and stack traces
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- App version
- Crash timestamps

**Firebase Analytics collects:**
- App opens and session duration
- Device type and OS version
- General app usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
- No personal information, file names, or account details

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect any personal information about you (name, email, etc.)
- We do not access your music file contents or names
- We do not track which songs you listen to
- We do not access any files outside the folders you explicitly select
- We do not upload your music files or metadata to our servers

## Third-Party Services

Cloud Drive Player integrates with third-party services. When you use these services, their privacy policies apply:

### Microsoft OneDrive

When you connect to OneDrive:
- You authenticate directly with Microsoft
- We receive a temporary access token to read files from folders you select
- We only access audio files in the folders you choose
- Microsoft's Privacy Policy applies: https://privacy.microsoft.com/

**Permissions Used:**
- `Files.ReadWrite.All`: To access and stream your music files

### Google Drive

When you connect to Google Drive:
- You authenticate directly with Google
- We receive a temporary access token to read files from folders you select
- We only access audio files in the folders you choose
- Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Permissions Used:**
- `drive` scope: To access and stream your music files

### Firebase (Crashlytics and Analytics)

Cloud Drive Player uses Firebase services to improve app quality:

**What Firebase Collects:**
- **Crash Reports:** Stack traces, device info, OS version when the app crashes
- **Analytics:** App usage statistics (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
- **Device Information:** Model, manufacturer, OS version, screen size
- **Performance Data:** App startup time, screen loading times

**What Firebase Does NOT Collect:**
- Your personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Your music file names, artists, or albums
- Your listening history or preferences
- Your OneDrive or Google Drive account information
- Contents of your music files

**Purpose:**
- Identify and fix crashes quickly
- Understand which features are most used
- Improve app performance and stability

**Data Retention:**
- Crash data: 90 days
- Analytics data: 14 months

**Third-Party Policy:**
- Google Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

**Note:** Firebase data collection cannot currently be disabled. If you prefer not to share this data, please do not use this app.

## How We Use Your Information

All information stored by Cloud Drive Player is used solely to provide you with music playback functionality:

- **Music Metadata:** To display your library and organize your music
- **Playback State:** To remember your queue and resume playback
- **Settings:** To maintain your preferences across app launches
- **Authentication Tokens:** To stream music from your cloud storage
- **Cache:** To improve playback performance and enable offline listening

## Data Storage and Security

- **All data is stored locally** on your device
- Authentication tokens are stored using Android's built-in encrypted storage
- We do not transmit any data to external servers (except authentication to Microsoft/Google)
- Your music files are streamed directly from your cloud storage to your device

## Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

### Access and Deletion
- All your data is stored on your device
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- You can sign out to delete cached files and authentication tokens
- You can clear cache and offline downloads from app settings

### Permissions Control
- You can revoke access to OneDrive/Google Drive at any time through:
  - Microsoft Account: https://account.microsoft.com/privacy/app-access
  - Google Account: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

### What Happens When You Uninstall
- All local data is deleted (library metadata, settings, cache)
- Your files on OneDrive/Google Drive remain untouched
- Authentication tokens are revoked

## Children's Privacy

Cloud Drive Player does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated for "Everyone" and does not contain any age-restricted content or features.

## Data Sharing

We share limited data with the following third parties:

**Google Firebase:**
- Crash reports and analytics data as described above
- Used solely to improve app stability and performance
- No personal information or music data is shared

**We do NOT share:**
- Your music library or listening history
- Your personal information
- Your cloud storage account details
- Any data with advertisers or marketing companies

## International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers involved, except:
- Authentication with Microsoft/Google servers (based on your region)
- Streaming audio files from your cloud storage (based on your storage location)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Requiring re-acceptance if changes are material

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your information is:

- **Consent:** You provide consent when selecting folders to access
- **Contract:** Processing is necessary to provide the music streaming service
- **Legitimate Interests:** Caching and performance optimization

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected (see above)
- Delete your personal information (uninstall the app)
- Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell any data)
