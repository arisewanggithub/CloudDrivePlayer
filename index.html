Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an internet connection to use Cloud Drive Player?

You need internet for streaming, but you can download folders for offline playback. Smart caching keeps your recent tracks available offline automatically.

Which cloud storage services are supported?

Currently OneDrive and Google Drive. You can use one cloud provider at a time and switch between them anytime.

What audio formats does it support?

MP3, AAC, FLAC, OGG Vorbis, WAV, OPUS, and WMA. Full support for high-quality lossless formats like FLAC.

Is my music secure?

Yes. Your cloud credentials are stored securely on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We don't have access to your music files or cloud account.

Does it work with Android Auto?

Yes! Full Android Auto integration with custom layout for safe driving. Control playback from your car's display.
